Split Atoms!

Build your own particle detector.

Particle Clicker

A roll-your-own from the authors of Particle Clicker

First, we will give a short overview of how particle detectors work and explain why they're useful to study physics.

Next we will guide you through creating your own detector visualization with Javascript and HTML5.

Starting from a blank HTML canvas, we will introduce detector components and physics concepts one by one, until your detector is complete.
