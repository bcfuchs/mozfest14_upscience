Geotag-X

Following the PyBossa presentation we'll look at GeoTag-X, built on PyBossa, focused specifically on crowdsourcing information for humanitarian disaster responses.

First, we'll take you through some GeoTag-X projects to give you a feel for how it works.

Next, you can have a go at designing your own projects. Or you can help us to improve Geotag-X by developing some of the features and enhancements we need, or brainstorm some new features!
